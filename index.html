About the Property

A Serene Alentejo Monte Just for You

Monte das Varginhas is set in a natural, authentic Alentejo landscape — completely private, beautifully peaceful. Your villa offers a private entrance and exclusive use of all outdoor spaces.

Just 2 km from the pristine beaches of the Costa Vicentina and 5 km from charming Sines, it's the perfect base for exploring Portugal's most unspoiled coastline. Relax in the shade of the ancient centenary chaparro — one of the estate's most iconic features.
